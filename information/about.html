About Hanmer Springs, New Zealand

Surrounded by beautiful mountains, and home to the famous Natural Hot Springs, Hanmer Springs is a must visit for many visitors both local and international.

Hanmer Springs is located 130km north of Christchurch in North Canterbury.

Hanmer Springs is known for its world class thermal pools complex, spa, clean fresh air and the many fantastic activities to keep you entertained. From relaxing in the pools to bungy jumping there is something here for everyone.

Learn more about Hanmer Springs and the many reasons that you should visit below.

Hanmer Springs today

Hanmer Springs is a popular tourist destination, and the town's population swells during holidays. Bungy jumping, jet boating, mountain biking and bush walking are common tourist activities, and there are helicopter tours available for the viewing of the town's alpine surroundings.

Hanmer Springs village

Around 1 million visitors are drawn to Hanmer Springs every year. Visitors come to experience our famous hot pools, search for adventure, seek out relaxation and rejuvenation, or to just breathe our pure mountain air.

Hanmer Springs activities

Hanmer Springs famous for its Thermal Waters is also known for many adventure activities including bungy jumping, jetboating, quadbiking, rafting.

The region is also home to plenty of hiking and biking trails, guided tours, family activities, scenic flights, spas and more.

Hanmer Springs events

Hanmer Springs plays host to a fantastic range of events all year round. Whether you’re into festivals and concerts, sporting events, wine and food, markets or family fun our Hanmer Springs events calendar will serve up something perfect for you. Many events are free, most are family-friendly.

Hanmer Springs weddings and conferences

The picturesque surroundings, lively atmosphere and great accommodation options make our alpine region a popular destination for weddings and conferences. From the surrounging mountains and farmlands to grape vines in a local vineyard, Hanmer Springs has a range of venues suitable for weddings or conferences.

There's also event planners, entertainers, hire companies and caterers located throughout the region to make planning your event easy.
