8 On Oregon

Located in tranquil surroundings and offering air-conditioned, boutique accommodation, 8 on Oregon adjoins the Heritage Forest and Conical Hill Walkway. Guests can enjoy stunning panoramic views of the village, Hanmer Basin and mountains.

Amuri Estate Retreat

Set on 26 acres, Amuri Estate Retreat & Olive Grove offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and included breakfast. There is a guest lounge with a fireplace and a large patio offering stunning mountain and river views.

Braemar Lodge & Spa

Select Braemar Lodge & Spa is a luxurious 5-star lodge nestled in the foothills of the Southern Alps, close to the village of Hanmer Springs.

Book Now

Drifters Inn

Offering free Wi-Fi, a sunny terrace and a guest lounge with a fireplace, Drifters Inn just 80 metres from the famous Hanmer Springs Thermal Reserve. It offers rooms with flat-screen satellite TV.

Hot Springs Motor Lodge

Hot Springs Motor Lodge is located just 800 metres from the famous Hanmer Springs Thermal Reserve, Hanmer Springs villa shops and a 3-minute drive from Hanmer Springs Golf Course. Free Wi-Fi and free off street car parking are available. Five Stags Restaurant Bar is located on site.

Book Now

Rippinvale Country Retreat

A very special New Zealand experience offering fine boutique accommodation in a comfortable and relaxed atmosphere. Rippinvale Retreat, constructed from adobe mud blocks, sits quietly in the natural Hanmer Springs alpine landscape enjoying spectacular views.

River View Lodge

Riverview Lodge is a unique brown trout fly fishing Lodge, located at Hamner Springs in the South Island of New Zealand. Robin and John at Riverview Lodge offer fly fishing packages and wonderful lodge accommodation.

Tussock Peak Lodge

Tussock Peak Motor Lodge offers modern suites, just 2 minutes walk from the famous Hanmer Springs Thermal Reserve. Guests receive 2 hours of free Wi-Fi access per day.
