Albergo Hanmer Lodge & Alpine Villa

Albergo Hanmer Lodge & Alpine Villa offers luxury accommodation, only 4 minutes drive from the renowned Hanmer Springs Thermal Reserve. Gourmet breakfast is included each morning.

Book Now

Annerleigh Bed & Breakfast

Guests at Annerleigh Luxury Bed and Breakfast can enjoy sitting on the wide verandahs to read, enjoy a glass of wine or just soak up the views. Free Wi-Fi and cooked or continental-style breakfasts are included.

Book Now

Cheltenham House

Cheltenham House offers B&B accommodation with free Wi-Fi in a quiet central location. Guests can enjoy a soak in the outdoor hot tub or a game of billiards by the guest lounge's open fireplace.

Book Now

Mad Cow Manor

Boasting rooms with a private patio and lovely mountain views, Mad Cow Manor offers adults-only accommodation, located just 10 minutes drive from Hanmer Springs. The price includes continental and cooked breakfast.

Book Now

Marchmont Gardens Bed & Breakfast

Rooms at Marchmont Gardens offer a patio with uninterrupted views of the mountains. Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast in the garden or in the comfort of their own rooms. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Book Now

Mira Monte Homestay

Surrounded by 5 acres of lovely gardens, Mira Monte B&B and Homestay offers free WiFi, free breakfast and free bicycle rental. It offers rooms with a private patio and views of the mountains and gardens.

Book Now

Rosies Bed & Breakfast

Just 3 minutes walk from Hanmer Springs Thermal Reserve, Rosie's Bed and Breakfast offers rooms with free continental breakfast. Set in lovely gardens, it features a guest lounge with fireplace and 42-inch flat-screen TV.

Book Now
